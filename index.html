A Place Where Faith
Meets Community

We are a Christ-centered community dedicated to sharing God's love, fostering spiritual growth, and empowering lives through faith. Everyone is welcome here — come as you are.

Let us rise up and build — we are committed to building God's Kingdom by creating a welcoming space where everyone can experience His love and find purpose in Him. — Nehemiah 2:18

Whether you are new to faith or a long-time believer, there is a place for you here at Oasis of Love in Portsmouth.

Our Foundations

Built on Eternal Truth

Three pillars guide everything we do — our Vision, Mission, and Statement of Faith.

🌅

Our Vision

To be a thriving, Christ-centered community that transforms lives and impacts Portsmouth through God's transforming love and grace.

🤝

Our Mission

To share the Gospel, nurture spiritual growth, and serve our community with compassion, excellence, and the love of Christ.

📖

Statement of Faith

We believe in the fundamental truths of Christianity as revealed in the Bible — our ultimate authority for faith and practice.

Men's Fellowship

The Men's Ministry at Oasis of Love is dedicated to empowering and inspiring men to grow in their faith, build strong relationships, and serve the community. Through fellowship, study, and outreach, we strive to equip men to be leaders in their homes, churches, and workplaces, walking in purpose and strength.

Ladies Ministry

The Ladies' Ministry at Oasis of Love is committed to nurturing and empowering women to grow in their faith, build lasting relationships, and serve others with love and compassion. Through fellowship, mentorship, and outreach, we encourage women to embrace their God-given purpose and live out their calling in every area of life.

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry at Oasis of Love is dedicated to inspiring and equipping young people to live boldly in their faith, build meaningful relationships, and make a positive impact in their communities. Through engaging activities, mentorship, and spiritual growth, we empower the next generation to stand firm in their beliefs and follow God’s purpose for their lives.

Couples Ministry

The Couples Ministry at Oasis of Love exists to encourage and strengthen marriages through faith, connection, and community. We believe healthy relationships are built on love, trust, and a shared commitment to God. Whether you're newly married or have been together for decades, our heart is to walk alongside couples as they grow together-through honest conversations, fellowship with others, and biblical guidance that keeps Christ at the centre.

Children's Ministry

The Children’s Ministry at Oasis of Love is passionate about nurturing the faith of young hearts, teaching them the love of God in fun and engaging ways. Through interactive lessons, activities, and Bible stories, we aim to help children grow in their understanding of God's word and develop a strong foundation of faith that will guide them throughout their lives.
